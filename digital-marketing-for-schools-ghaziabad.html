Education Marketing FAQ

Questions Schools & Institutes Actually Ask

Why does a well-established school in Ghaziabad need digital marketing if it already has a good reputation?+
With over 450 schools in Ghaziabad, reputation alone doesn't guarantee visibility to parents who are new to an area or comparing options online. A strong digital presence ensures your reputation is actually found by the parents searching for a school like yours.
How can a newer school compete with established names like DPS or Delhi Public School in search results?+
Established brands dominate broad searches, but a newer school can win highly specific searches — your locality plus board plus grade level — through focused local SEO, genuine parent reviews and consistent content that builds trust over time.
When should a school start its admission marketing campaign?+
Ideally 3 to 4 months before your admission window opens. This gives Google Ads and SEO enough time to build momentum, and lets parents research and shortlist schools well before the deadline rush.
Is Google Ads or Meta Ads more effective for school admission campaigns?+
Google Ads captures parents actively searching for a school in your locality, which tends to convert faster. Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram) work well for broader brand awareness and reaching parents who aren't actively searching yet but fit your target profile.
How do you help a coaching institute compete for students preparing for exams like JEE or NEET?+
Coaching institutes need highly specific SEO and ad targeting around the exact exam and locality, plus credible content like faculty credentials and past result trends, since students and parents compare institutes carefully before enrolling.
Can digital marketing help a preschool or play school in a residential area of Ghaziabad?+
Yes, and often very effectively. Preschools benefit strongly from hyperlocal Google Business Profile optimisation and Instagram content, since parents typically search for options within a very short distance from home.
How do you measure the success of a school's digital marketing campaign?+
Primarily through tracked enquiries, campus visit bookings and cost per admission lead — not just website traffic or social media followers, which don't directly reflect admission growth.
How much does digital marketing cost for a school or coaching institute in Ghaziabad?+
Costs vary by institution size and admission season intensity — a single-campus school has different needs than a coaching chain with multiple centres. We provide a specific quote after a free audit rather than a fixed price list.
Does a school really need a new website, or can social media alone handle admissions?+
Social media builds awareness, but most parents still expect to verify curriculum, fees, facilities and faculty on a proper website before enquiring — especially for a decision as significant as their child's education.
Do you offer a free marketing audit for schools and educational institutes?+
Yes. We review your website, Google Business Profile and current ad activity, then show you specifically what parents see when they search for a school or institute like yours in Ghaziabad, at no cost and with no obligation.
