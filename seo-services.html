Why Most SEO Retainers Don't Show Results

The Problem Isn't SEO. It's How It's Usually Sold.

We've taken over more than a few SEO accounts from previous agencies in Ghaziabad and Delhi NCR, and the pattern is almost always the same: a monthly report full of keyword rankings for terms nobody actually searches, a backlink count with no mention of where those links came from, and traffic numbers with no breakdown of whether that traffic ever turned into an enquiry. The work looked busy. It just wasn't connected to anything the business cared about.

The fix isn't more SEO activity, it's tying every action back to a number that matters before any work starts. If a keyword ranks but nobody in Ghaziabad is searching it, ranking for it is wasted effort. If a backlink comes from a low-quality directory, it's not worth the time it took to acquire. We'd rather rank you for fifteen keywords that bring in real enquiries than three hundred that bring in nothing.

That also means being upfront about timeline. A website with a clean technical foundation and some existing authority can see movement in 6-8 weeks. A site with structural issues, thin content, and no backlink profile is realistically a 6-month project before rankings reflect the work. We'll tell you which situation you're in during the audit, not after you've paid for three months of retainer.

Common Questions

SEO FAQ

How much does SEO cost in Ghaziabad?+
SEO pricing depends on your website's current state, competition, and how many locations or services you need to rank for. Most Ghaziabad and Delhi NCR businesses start with a monthly retainer covering technical fixes, content, and link building. Book a free audit for a quote specific to your goals.
How long does SEO take to show results?+
SEO is a long-term strategy. Most websites start seeing measurable ranking improvements within 3-6 months, with significant traffic and lead growth building over 6-12 months as topical authority and backlinks accumulate.
What is the difference between SEO and Google Ads?+
SEO builds long-term organic rankings that don't require ongoing ad spend but take months to show results. Google Ads delivers immediate visibility through paid placements where you pay per click. Most businesses use both together for faster, compounding growth.
Do you guarantee first-page rankings?+
No ethical SEO agency can guarantee specific rankings, since Google's algorithm considers hundreds of factors and competitors are also optimizing. We focus on proven white-hat strategies that consistently improve rankings and organic traffic over time.
What is local SEO and do I need it?+
Local SEO optimizes your visibility for location-based searches like "near me" queries and Google Maps results. If you serve customers in a specific area like Ghaziabad or Indirapuram, local SEO is essential for appearing in local search results.
Do you perform a free SEO audit before starting?+
Yes. Every engagement starts with a free SEO audit covering technical issues, on-page gaps, and competitor benchmarking, so you know exactly what's being fixed and why before committing to ongoing SEO.
What is included in monthly SEO reporting?+
You receive transparent monthly reports covering keyword rankings, organic traffic, technical fixes completed, and content or link-building activity — with clear recommendations for the following month.
Can SEO help my e-commerce or enterprise website?+
Yes. We offer dedicated e-commerce SEO for product and category page optimization, and enterprise SEO for larger websites that need scalable technical fixes and content structures across many pages.
Does SEO work differently for a local Ghaziabad business versus a pan-India one?+
Yes. A local business needs local SEO and Google Business Profile optimization focused on a specific area. A business selling across India needs broader content and authority building with little local-search component. We scope the strategy around which one you actually are.
What SEO tools do you use?+
We use a combination of Google Search Console, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Google Business Profile, along with industry tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, and Screaming Frog for technical audits and competitor research.
