Why Most Social Accounts Plateau at the Same Follower Count

Posting Consistently Isn't the Same as Growing.

A lot of the businesses that come to us in Ghaziabad and Delhi NCR have already been posting for a year or more — three times a week, like every guide says to. The follower count just hasn't moved much, and neither have enquiries. The posting habit was never the missing piece. What was missing was a reason for anyone outside their existing customers to stop scrolling and pay attention.

Reach on Instagram and Facebook now comes disproportionately from Reels and Stories that get shared, not from feed posts that get liked by people who already follow you. A polished product photo with a caption gets seen by your existing audience. A Reel that actually says something — shows a process, answers a real question, pokes fun at an industry cliché — gets shown to people who've never heard of you. Most accounts that plateau are producing content built for the first kind of growth while expecting the second kind of result.

That's why our retainers include actual production, not just a content calendar. A documented strategy is worth very little if the Reels behind it are shot on a phone with bad lighting and no hook in the first two seconds. We'd rather post less often with content that's actually built to be watched than hit a quota of posts nobody finishes.

Common Questions

Social Media Marketing FAQ

How much does social media marketing cost in Ghaziabad?+
Costs depend on the number of platforms, content volume, and whether paid promotion is included. Most Ghaziabad and Delhi NCR brands start with a monthly retainer covering content creation, posting, and community management. Book a free audit for a quote specific to your goals.
Which social media platform is best for my business?+
It depends on your audience. Instagram and Facebook work well for most local and consumer businesses, LinkedIn is stronger for B2B companies, and YouTube suits brands that can commit to regular video content. We recommend the right mix after understanding your business and audience.
How often should I post on social media?+
Consistency matters more than frequency. Most brands see good results posting 3-5 times a week on Instagram and Facebook, supported by daily Stories and regular Reels, rather than posting daily without a clear content strategy.
Do you create the content or just manage posting?+
We handle the full process — content strategy, creative design, Reels and video production, copywriting, posting, scheduling, and community management. Many of our retainers also include professional shoots for product, brand, and event content.
How long before I see results from social media marketing?+
Engagement and reach improvements are often visible within the first month. Meaningful follower growth and lead generation typically build over 3-6 months as content strategy, consistency, and community trust compound.
Do you run paid social media ads as well?+
Yes. Alongside organic content and community management, we run targeted social media advertising on Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn to accelerate reach, engagement, and lead generation.
Do you need to visit our location for shoots, or can content be planned remotely?+
Shoots happen on-site since they need your actual products, space, or team, but strategy calls, content review, and scheduling all run remotely. Most ongoing retainers only need an in-person shoot day once every few weeks, with everything else handled over calls.
Do you offer professional photo and video shoots?+
Yes. We offer professional Reels production, product photography, brand shoots, and event coverage as part of our content creation process, so your social presence looks consistently premium.
What is included in your social media reporting?+
You receive transparent monthly reports covering reach, engagement, follower growth, top-performing content, and lead generation where applicable — with clear recommendations for the following month.
