What We Actually Check Before Quoting a Website

Most Website Quotes Skip the Question That Matters Most.

Almost every website enquiry we get in Ghaziabad starts the same way: "I need a website like [competitor]." That's a reasonable starting point, but it skips the actual question — what is this website supposed to do once it's live? A site built to look impressive and a site built to fill a calendar with consultation bookings are structured completely differently, even if they end up using similar colors and fonts.

So before we quote anything, we ask what happens after someone lands on the homepage. Are they calling, filling a form, browsing a product catalogue, or just checking you're a real business before visiting in person? That answer decides where the call-to-action sits, how many steps the contact form has, and whether you even need more than three pages. Most of the "redesign" requests we get aren't really design problems — they're websites that were built for browsing when the business actually needed a site built for converting.

We also won't quote a fixed price before seeing what you're starting with. A business website with clear content ready to go is a different scope from one where we're also writing copy, sourcing photography, and untangling a previous developer's half-finished WordPress install. We'd rather spend twenty minutes understanding the real starting point than hand you a number that changes three weeks into the project.

Common Questions

Web Development FAQ

How much does a business website cost in Ghaziabad?+
Cost depends on the number of pages, design complexity, and features like e-commerce or custom functionality. A basic business website costs less than a custom-coded e-commerce platform. Book a free audit and we'll give you a clear quote based on your specific requirements.
How long does it take to build a website?+
A standard business website typically takes 2-4 weeks from approved design to launch. Larger custom or e-commerce builds can take 6-8 weeks depending on complexity and how quickly content and feedback are provided.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?+
Yes. Every website we build is mobile-first by default, since most visitors in Ghaziabad and Delhi NCR browse on mobile devices first. We test across screen sizes before launch.
Do you build websites on WordPress or custom code?+
Both. We choose the right approach based on your needs — WordPress for content-heavy sites that need easy editing, or custom-coded HTML, CSS and JavaScript for performance-critical or highly bespoke projects.
Will my website be optimized for SEO?+
Yes. Every site we build follows SEO-friendly development practices — clean code, fast load times, proper heading structure, and mobile responsiveness — so it's ready to rank from day one.
Can you redesign my existing website?+
Yes. We offer full website redesign services that modernise your design, improve speed and mobile experience, and preserve your existing SEO rankings during the transition.
Do you offer website maintenance after launch?+
Yes. We offer ongoing website maintenance and support plans covering updates, backups, security monitoring, and minor content changes after your site goes live.
Can you build an e-commerce website for my business?+
Yes. We build e-commerce websites on platforms suited to your catalogue size and growth plans, including product management, secure checkout, and payment gateway integration.
How do you make websites load faster?+
We optimize images, minimise code, use efficient hosting configurations, and follow performance best practices throughout development, then run speed audits before launch to confirm fast load times.
Does it matter if my business is only registered in Ghaziabad but I want to sell pan-India?+
Not for the build itself, but it changes what the site needs. A Ghaziabad-only business needs strong local signals like address and service-area pages, while a pan-India site needs broader navigation and shipping or service-coverage information instead. We scope this during requirement discussion, not after the design is done.
